DAILY BLOG

Half a Year Halfway Around the World
Posted on November 15, 2012
Six months. Six months. Six months. Those words have been on repeat for the past two days. Six months since I’ve seen the love of my life. Day 200 is creeping up really fast, and I know that should comfort me; you know, that it’s going by quickly. It makes me scared, though, and sad, and jealous of everyone that doesn’t have to wait months to see the one they love.
What if he doesn’t love me after being away from me for so long? What if I’m not what he wants anymore? That’s the petty side of me… The more serious and concerned side of me worries because he’s now in transition and therefore out of contact. What if I don’t hear from him in two-three weeks when I expect him to be at his new location? What if something happens to him?
Six months in and the worry only grows greater with every day.
